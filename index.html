How to Play

Starting the Game
Select between ENDLESS and Normal Mode by clicking the corresponding button. This will redirect you
to the game page and you can start WHACKing!

ENDLESS
When the game begins, it begins and ends at NOTHING! The game will continue until you fail to reach
the target score in time! So be mindfull of the timer!

Normal Mode
If you reach the score within the time, you move on to the next round! Be on the lookout for the moles
can trick you!

Have Fun!
